ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

We aim to make our app accessible without barriers. It should be designed to be in compliance with national legislation implementing Directive (EU) 2016/2102 of the European Parliament. The following accessibility statement applies to the Citykey apps for Android and iOS.

Status of compatibility with respect to the requirements for barrier-free information technology

This app is currently not yet fully compatible with the requirements according to BITV. The accessibility of the app is undergoing constant improvements as part of our development process. 

Non-accessible content

Contrast

There is currently some content for which the contrast is too low. We are currently looking into how we can increase the contrast in line with Citykey's color scheme.

Button size

Some clickable objects (e.g. buttons, links) in the Android app may be a bit too small for users with motor weaknesses. We are currently investigating to what extent changes to the size are possible and useful.

Dynamic text size in iOS

Citykey for iOS does not support dynamic text sizes at the moment. We are currently planning to successively support this feature throughout the apps.

Creation of this accessibility statement

This statement was created on July 30, 2021. The assessment is based on self-assessment, with the support of accessibility testing software tools, and with the assistance of a visually impaired user.

Feedback and contact

We appreciate feedback on the accessibility of our app. Have you discovered any other barriers in our app or do you have any questions about accessibility? Then feel free to contact us at citykey-support@telekom.de.
